Menu of Staff Meal

DEMO

https://cchin01.github.io/Yahoo-Menu/

Target Audience

Company employees who are accustomed to eating employee meals every single day.

Pain Points

The menu layout diagram provided by the lunch supplier cooperating with the company is as follows:

Original Menu layout

In other words, when I want to compare the dishes of each supplier on Wednesday to decide what I want to eat today, I have to go back and forth through five pages.

Only 1/5 of the menu can be seen on each page.

How to solve the problem?

Allow users to filter by "day of week" as a dimension to facilitate comparison of different lunches on the same day.

Organize requirements

This is the evaluation method used by baremetrics to measure product functions.

Wireframe

MVP

  • Because there are 6 dishes in a day, the general screen is 6 items a row on the CSS setting, and 3 items a row on the mobile device.
  • There is only one choice for salads and light meals a day, so it's a filter option on its own.
